Spicy Pad Thai


Cook your noodle of choice.
In a pan, add two tablespoons of coconut oil and one tablespoon of sesame oil. Heat.
Add 3-5 hot thai chilies, 3 garlic cloves, and 6 inches of green onion cut into small circles.
Drop in the meat of choice.
Once the meat is almost cooked, cut and cook green and red peppers and a handful of cabbage into the pan
Once that's done, it's time for two tablespoons of liquid aminos and one tablespoon of fish sauce.
Finally a dash of Truvia Sweet Complete Brown and a bit of hot chili oil, or a lot.
