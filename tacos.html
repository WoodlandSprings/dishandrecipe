Tacos


Pick up quality cod from the super market and defrost
In a pan, melt butter.
Add grape seed oil, garlic, onion powder, salt, and oregano.
Cook the cod until nearly transparent.
Lay it over corn tortilla (heat the tortillas in a dry pan first until pliable).
Add a quick slaw.
Drizzle a hot sauce over top. I prefer Lousiana Cajun or Franks.
